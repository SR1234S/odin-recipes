Best Fluffy Pancakes

Description

The perfect pancakes to make for a Sunday brunch or just for fun. They're soft, fluffy, and perfect.

There is a certain level of pancake maker skill needed to make these, so listen closely. The batter can't be too thick or too runny. Run your batter down the side of the rim and watch if it goes slowly down the side of the mixing bowl. Thats how you'll know you're about to make the best fluffy pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose or plain flour
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda*
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar or sweetener
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¾ cups milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
